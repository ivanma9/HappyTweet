Happy Scores by state

This is a choropleth map of happiness scores assigned to different US cities. The data represented was collected by Wallethub from various reports and surveys reporting different measures reflecting happiness. The researchers assigned weights to each measure analyzed to calculate an overall happiness score for each city. The map represents average happiness scores of the multiple cities recorded for each state. Each state is categorized into different colors based off these scores. Red colors represent lower average happiness scores, meaning lower levels of happiness, and green scores represent higher happiness scores, meaning higher levels of happiness.
